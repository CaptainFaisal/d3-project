    Covid-19 Horrors

    The pandemic has had a profound impact on the pharmaceutical sector. It has hastened the adoption of digital technologies, spurred increased investment in research and development, and brought a renewed focus on combating infectious diseases. Pharmaceutical firms have responded by enhancing flexibility in operations and engaging customers through digital channels. They've also faced challenges such as interrupted supply chains and postponed clinical trials. Ultimately, the crisis highlighted the industry's ability to innovate and adapt.
